In September, Coinbase open-sourced Toshi, their in-house Bitcoin full node for querying the Bitcoin blockchain and broadcasting transactions, powered by Ruby and PostgreSQL. Compared to Bitcoin Core, it allows much richer SQL querying at the expense of a much larger data store (220GB vs 25GB as of September).
